The Doomguy, commonly known as the Marine and referred to as the Doom Slayer in the rebooted portion of the series, is the protagonist in the Doom video game franchise of first-person shooters created by id Software, and its sequels and spin-off media. He is a space marine dressed in green combat armor who does not speak onscreen.
